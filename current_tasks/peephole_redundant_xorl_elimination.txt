Task: Eliminate redundant xorl %eax, %eax instructions in peephole optimizer
Started: 2026-02-04

Problem: The accumulator-based codegen generates patterns like:
  xorl %eax, %eax
  movq %rax, -N(%rbp)
  xorl %eax, %eax          # redundant - rax is already zero
  movq %rax, -M(%rbp)

The second xorl is redundant because movq %rax, -N(%rbp) doesn't modify %rax.

This pattern appears ~4000 times in sqlite3.c's VdbeExec function alone.

Fix: Add a pattern in combined_local_pass that tracks when %rax is known to be zero
and skips redundant xorl %eax, %eax instructions.
