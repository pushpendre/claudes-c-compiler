Status: in_progress

Split all 4 backend codegen.rs files into logical submodules.

The 4 codegen files are the largest in the codebase:
- x86/codegen/codegen.rs: 4371 lines
- i686/codegen/codegen.rs: 4488 lines
- arm/codegen/codegen.rs: 4107 lines
- riscv/codegen/codegen.rs: 3356 lines

Plan: Split each into focused submodules (extracting from the monolithic codegen.rs):
- stack_frame.rs: calculate_stack_space, prologue, epilogue, store_params, param_ref
- memory.rs: load/store, GEP, memcpy, typed store/load, alloca helpers, copy_value
- integer.rs: int binop, comparison, unary ops, fused cmp branch, select
- i128_ops.rs: i128 arithmetic, shifts, comparison, divrem, float conversion
- calls.rs: call ABI config, stack/reg args, call instruction, cleanup, store result
- casts.rs: emit_cast, emit_cast_instrs (where not already split)
- variadic.rs: va_arg, va_start, va_copy, va_arg_struct
- float.rs: float neg, cmp, binop, second-return-value helpers
- atomics.rs: atomic RMW, CAS, load, store, fence (where not already split)
- control_flow.rs: branch, switch, jump table, global addr, label addr, TLS

Core struct, constructor, and register helpers stay in codegen.rs.
